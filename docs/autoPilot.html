Motivation

Following my racing drone, I began developing my flying experience with fixed-wing aircraft. Using resources available from the community (designs, tips, etc.), I built and learned to fly the aircraft pictured on this page.

After taking more advanced controls courses, I was inspired to design an autopilot system and implement it in hardware. The goal I envisioned was to define a sequence of waypoints for the autopilot to track and navigate in addition to launching and landing assistance and automation.

After a proposal to my professor, I took this on as my capstone Senior Design Project, the culmination of the Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering Major at Cornell. My detailed 32 page report is linked below.
